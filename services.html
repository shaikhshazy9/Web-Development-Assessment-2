A Journey Beyond Expectations

At Etihad Airways, we believe your journey should be as memorable as your destination. From the moment you book until you reach your final destination, we're committed to providing exceptional service that caters to your every need.

Discover the range of services we offer to make your travel experience seamless, comfortable, and enjoyable.

First Class

Ultimate Comfort in the Sky

We've designed our cabin experience to ensure maximum comfort, regardless of which class you fly. Enjoy extra legroom, adjustable headrests, and ambient lighting in Economy, or experience the luxury of our Business and First Class cabins.

First Class

Private suites with sliding doors, Poltrona Frau leather seating that converts to a fully-flat bed, and premium bedding.

Business Class

Spacious seats that convert to fully-flat beds with direct aisle access, plush pillows, and premium blankets.

Economy Class

Ergonomically designed seats with adjustable headrests, ample legroom, and amenity kits on long-haul flights.

Amenities

Luxury toiletries, noise-cancelling headphones, and premium sleepwear in premium cabins.
