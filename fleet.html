Our Boeing 777-300ER aircraft feature spacious cabins with enhanced comfort across all classes. First Class offers private suites, Business Class provides full-flat beds, and Economy Class ensures a comfortable journey with adjustable headrests and ample legroom.

Airbus A380

Capacity Up to 496 passengers

Cabin Features

First Class - Ultimate Luxury

Experience unparalleled luxury in our First Class cabin. Each private suite offers complete privacy and features a 6'8" fully-flat bed, a personal wardrobe, and a vanity unit. Enjoy gourmet dining with our Dine Anytime service, featuring dishes created by world-renowned chefs.

  • Private suite with sliding doors
  • 32-inch TV with over 100 movies
  • Personal chef and butler service
  • Designer sleepwear and amenity kits
  • Shower facilities (A380 only)

Business Class - Refined Comfort

Our Business Class offers a perfect blend of productivity and relaxation. The ergonomically designed seats convert into fully-flat beds, while direct aisle access ensures convenience for all passengers. Enjoy a premium dining experience with our Business Class menu.

  • Fully-flat bed up to 80 inches long
  • 18.5-inch touchscreen TV
  • In-seat power outlet and USB port
  • Premium noise-canceling headphones
  • Exclusive lounge access

Economy Class - Comfort for All

Our Economy Class cabin offers a comfortable journey with ergonomically designed seats featuring adjustable headrests and ample legroom. Enjoy our award-winning entertainment system and a variety of meal options catering to different dietary requirements.

  • Comfortable seats with up to 32-inch pitch
  • 11.6-inch touchscreen TV
  • USB charging port
  • Complimentary blankets and pillows
  • Multiple meal options
